Context:

You are an HR recruiter who specializes in the fashion industry. I own a menswear company called Elk Head Clothing. I am hiring someone to help manage the business. The overarching objective for this role is to help craft a strategic vision for Elk Head Clothing and to drive the company to accomplish its goals. 

‍

Why this role is exciting:

  • Elk Head is already a successful brand, and this is an opportunity to make it into something truly special.
  • You will be working directly with the founder to craft a company strategy and accomplish it together. 
  • As the brand grows, this role will grow commensurately 
  • You are encouraged to take this role and make it your own. As long as you are driving excellence, you will have the founder’s support and enthusiasm.


Candidate requirements:

  • Background: While the background requirements for this role are flexible, the ideal candidate will be able to demonstrate success in previous roles or academic pursuits. A positive outlook and professional disposition are required.  
  • Organized: Must be systematic in their approach; Able to prioritize strategic initiatives and accomplish them; Must maintain production calendar, content calendar, and keep the entire organization on track. 
  • Focused: Driven to accomplish goals and high priority tasks successfully and without distraction.
  • Entrepreneurial: Constantly looking for ways to refine and grow the brand. 
  • Scrappy: Creative in their approach and willing to get their hands dirty
  • Ambitious: Highly motivated to grow the company


Among other things, this person will be responsible for: 

Brand:
  • Assist with a comprehensive brand refresh, defining and executing an updated vision for the brand. 
  • Work with the founder to define all aspects of the Elk Head Brand; Devise strategies to bring the Elk Head lifestyle to life, including look and feel, tone of voice, store 
Product:
  • Assist with demand forecasting and product strategy 
  • Manage production calendars to ensure on-time production and delivery of products 
  • Assist with the management of production logistics, assisting with purchasing, sampling, and delivery of finished goods.
  • Arrange product photography for website and social media.
  • Assist with product content creation, product descriptions, etc. 
  • Production vendor management 
Marketing:
  • Assist with the development of marketing goals to drive customer acquisition and retention 
  • Facilitate accomplishment of marketing goals by hiring and managing third party vendors 
  • Manage advertising budget 
Physical store strategy: 
  • Devise strategies to optimize the performance of the store
  • Implement stronger inventory management 
  • Ensure the store upholds and extends the Elk Head brand. 
Communications and outreach:
  • Craft and execute a press strategy 
  • Plan local events and outreach
  • Create opportunities to collaborate with other brands and to form strategic partnerships
  • Assist in content calendar creation and management, ensuring that social media, email and website content are managed consistently and effectively. 
  • Implement and maintain customer relationship management best practices. 

‍

What this role isn’t:

  • While having a great sense of style would be a bonus, the ability to design clothing and/or marketing graphics is not required. The candidate will be responsible for product and brand strategy, but not for designing actual products or brand identity elements. 

‍

Role specifications: 

  • Location: The working arrangements for this role are flexible, and strong candidates would have the option of working remotely. 
  • Engagement model: This will initially be a part-time role, paying $2,000 monthly. Ideally, a successful candidate would expand this role, make it their own, and turn it into a full-time opportunity. Exceptional candidates may also have an opportunity to receive equity in the business. 

Application process:

Please submit resumes to garrett@elkheadclothing.com.
