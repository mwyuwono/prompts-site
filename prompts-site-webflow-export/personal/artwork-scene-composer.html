Changes Made
  • Edit: Replaced "very simple" with "straightforward" in the first point.
    Rationale: "Straightforward" conveys the idea more effectively and professionally, as "very simple" could potentially undermine the complexity of the task.
  • Edit: Replaced "purposes" with "objectives" in the second point.
    Rationale: "Objectives" is more commonly used in a business context, making it a more professional choice of words.
  • Edit: Replaced the contraction "it's" with "it is" in the last two points.
    Rationale: Formal writing generally avoids contractions, as they can be seen as informal.
